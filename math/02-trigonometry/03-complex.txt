Euler's formula
---
\[ e^{ix} = \cos x + i \sin x \]
\[ e^{-ix} = \cos x - i \sin x \]
===

De Moivre's formula
---
\[ \left( \cos(x) + i \sin(x) \right)^n = \cos(nx) + i \sin(nx) \]
\[ \left( \cosh(x) + i \sinh(x) \right)^n = \cosh(nx) + i \sinh(nx) \]
===
